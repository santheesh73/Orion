# Code of Conduct

Orion follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, constructive, and collaborative.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming language.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the work and the user experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of project discussions.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation when needed to protect the project community.
